Become a PetResQ Volunteer

Join our dedicated team of animal lovers and make a real difference in the lives of rescue animals. Your time and skills can save lives.

Volunteer Opportunities

Animal Care

Help with feeding, grooming, socializing, and exercising our rescue animals. Perfect for animal lovers!

  • Feeding and watering
  • Grooming and bathing
  • Exercise and playtime
  • Socialization training

Foster Care

Provide temporary homes for animals awaiting adoption. We supply all necessary food and supplies.

  • Temporary housing
  • Basic training
  • Socialization
  • Medical care support

Event Support

Help organize and run adoption events, fundraisers, and community outreach programs.

  • Adoption events
  • Fundraising
  • Community outreach
  • Educational programs

Facility Support

Assist with cleaning, maintenance, and improvement projects at our shelter facilities.

  • Cleaning and sanitizing
  • Maintenance work
  • Landscaping
  • Repair projects

Volunteer Requirements

Age Requirements

Volunteers must be at least 16 years old. Volunteers under 18 need parental consent.

Time Commitment

We ask for a minimum commitment of 4 hours per week for at least 3 months.

Training

All volunteers complete orientation and role-specific training before starting.

Background Check

Some positions may require a background check for the safety of our animals.
